Why we made this website

Our app is meant for people who are eating at fast food restaurants who have dietary restricitons. Instead of having to take the time to look up individual items from the menue online, the user will have their options listed for them based on the restauruant they are eating at and their dietary restriction. While this website is just a prototype, the finished product of this website would have many more restaurant options and food items per restaurant.
